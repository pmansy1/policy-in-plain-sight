About Us

Our mission and vision for accessible policy information

Our Mission

PoliCode was founded with a clear mission: to make policy information accessible to everyone, regardless of educational background or prior knowledge of legal systems.

We believe that understanding the laws and policies that affect our daily lives should not be restricted to those with advanced degrees or specialized training. By translating complex legal language into clear, understandable terms, we aim to empower communities that have historically been left out of policy conversations.

Our platform focuses particularly on serving low-income, Black, and Latino communities, who are often most impacted by policy changes but least informed about them.

Our Values

Accessibility

Making complex information accessible to everyone regardless of background.

Community Focus

Prioritizing the needs of underserved communities in all we do.

Accuracy

Ensuring all information is factual, verified, and unbiased.

Empowerment

Giving people the knowledge they need to advocate for themselves.
