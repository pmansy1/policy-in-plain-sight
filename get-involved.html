Ways to Get Involved

There are many ways to take action on policies that matter to you. PoliCode is here to help you find meaningful ways to get involved, whether you have just a few minutes or are ready for a more significant commitment.

Spread Awareness

Share articles and information about important policies with your network. Raising awareness is a powerful first step toward creating change.

  • Share PoliCode articles on social media
  • Discuss policies with friends and family
  • Forward our newsletter to interested contacts

Vote Informed

Understanding policies is crucial for making informed voting decisions. Use our resources to learn about what's on your ballot.

  • Check your voter registration status
  • Learn about upcoming elections
  • Research candidates' policy positions

Contact Representatives

Reaching out directly to elected officials can have a significant impact on policy decisions. Make your voice heard.

  • Find your representatives
  • Use our templates for emails or calls
  • Attend town halls and public meetings

Join Local Groups

Connect with others who care about the same issues. Community organizing can lead to powerful collective action.

  • Find community groups in your area
  • Attend local policy meetings
  • Participate in community initiatives

Volunteer with PoliCode

Help us make policy information more accessible to everyone. We're looking for volunteers with various skills and backgrounds to join our mission.

Current Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Policy Translators: Help simplify complex policy language
  • Community Outreach: Connect PoliCode with local communities
  • Content Contributors: Write or edit articles about policies
  • Event Coordinators: Organize educational events
  • Language Translators: Help translate content into multiple languages
